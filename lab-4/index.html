The below points are the key address for what I have used in styling:

1. Font Choices

▪ The primary font used is Montserrat, with weights 400 (regular), 500 (semi-bold), and 700 (bold).

▪ Additionally, sans-serif is specified as a fallback font for compatibility.

2. Heading and Text Sizes

▪ Headings are styled with a font size of 20 pixels and a font weight of 700, making them bold and larger for emphasis.

▪ The main text size is set to 16 pixels, ensuring readability and consistency.

▪ I have used h1 head tag for title because title tag should be h1 only

3. Color Choices

▪ Background colors:

▪ The main container (.cd__main) has a linear gradient background from a light blue (#c9d6ff) to a light gray (#e2e2e2).

▪ The body background is set to a darker grayish-blue (#585c68), providing contrast and readability.

▪ I chose these color preferences beacuse it is visually appealing and reader can read it clearly

▪ Text Colors:

▪ Bold text (.bold) is styled in white (#fff) against a blue background for visibility.

▪ Regular text (.regular) is presented in a lighter shade of blue (#b1eaff) for readability.

4. Margin and Padding Choices

The document as a whole:
▪ Utilizes a margin of 50 pixels on the top and bottom and auto margins on the left and right for centering the content.

Individual sections:
▪ Resume sections (.resume) have a width of 1000 pixels and utilize flexbox for layout.
▪ Left section (.resume_left) has a width of 580 pixels and a 50-pixel padding on the sides to provide space between content and container edges.
▪ Right section (.resume_right) has a width of 1220 pixels and a 25-pixels padding on the sides to provide content and edges.
▪ Resume items (.resume_item) have 25 pixels of padding on the top and bottom for spacing between items.

▪ For the map location icon, I used fas fa-map-signs.
